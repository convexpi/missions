# Lectures

Short, self-contained teaching notebooks (run them in Colab — badge at the top of each). They build
the statistical intuition the missions and the Lab's out-of-sample grading rely on.

| Notebook | Topic |
|---|---|
| `p_hacking.ipynb` | Multiple comparisons: why testing many signals manufactures false positives, and how out-of-sample testing catches it |
| `factor_evaluation.ipynb` | The Information Coefficient: measuring a signal's predictive power (rank IC, IC-IR, IC decay) before you backtest |

These are original, clean-room notebooks. Pair them with Mission 1 (overfitting) and Mission 3 (alpha discovery).
